Characters

Rebecca Chambers

Protagonist of the game. The Chemicals Expert and Medic of S.T.A.R.S Bravo Team. A gifted individual, she managed to graduate from university with a bachelor's in Chemistry at age 18. Due to being a rookie and youngest amongst her colleagues, most of the S.T.A.R.S members are protective of her.

She is also a fan of basketball and enjoys playing the sport in her free time.

Billy Coen

A former Marine sentenced to execution for murdering at least twenty-three people. He managed to escape from the Ecliptic Express during the chaos that took part the night Bravo Team arrived.

Albert Wesker

Captain of S.T.A.R.S Alpha Team. He is a very gifted individual with expertise in science.

Not much is known about his past. Although he managed to graduate from university with a doctorate at the age of seventeen. Knowledge of the first Resident Evil game is recommended.

William Birkin

One of Umbrella's top scientists and father of Sherry Birkin.
