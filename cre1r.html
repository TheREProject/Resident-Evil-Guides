Characters

Chris Redfield: Playable character 1

Sharpshooter and co-pilot of S.T.A.R.S Alpha Team. and former Air Force Pilot. It was here that he met Barry Burton and began a good friendship with him.

Eventually, they left the Air Force and joined S.T.A.R.S in 1996 where they met Jill Valentine.

Jill Valentine: Playable character 2

The B&E expert of S.T.A.R.S Alpha Team. Before joining the team, she was a member of US Army's Delta Force until 1996. SHe displayed remarkable feats of lock-picking and bomb disposal.

Her strong sense of justice, resiliance, and willingness to protect others is what led her to join S.T.A.R.S.

Albert Wesker

Captain of S.T.A.R.S Alpha Team. He is a very gifted individual with expertise in science.

Not much is known about his past. Although he managed to graduate from university with a doctorate at the age of seventeen.

Barry Burton

Weapons supervisor of S.T.A.R.S Alpha Team and former Air Force Pilot. It was here that he met Chris Redfield and began a good friendship with him. Eventually, he married a woman named Kathy and had two daughters with her, Moira and Polly.

Eventually, Barry and Chris left the Air Force and joined S.T.A.R.S in 1996 where they met Jill Valentine.

Rebecca Chambers

Chemicals Expert and Medic of S.T.A.R.S Bravo Team. A gifted individual, she managed to graduate from university with a bachelor's in Chemistry at age 18. Due to being a rookie and youngest amongst her colleagues, most of the S.T.A.R.S members are protective of her.

She is also a fan of basketball and enjoys playing the sport in her free time.
