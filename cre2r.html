Characters

Leon Scott Kennedy: Playable character 1

A 21-year-old Rookie Police Officer who graduated at the top of his class. As a child, he lost his parents due to their connection crime. His inspiration to become a police office came from the office who saved his life after his orphaning. His reasons for joining the RPD came from word of the murder cases occurring in the area. However, due to a drunken night, he arrived at the city later than expected. An act that may have saved his life.

Claire Redfield: Playable character 2

Chris Redfeild's younger sister. A 19-year-old college student who left for Raccoon City after weeks of no contact with her brother. After their parents' deaths, it was just her and Chris. Claire would eventually gain an interest in motorcycles and lockpicking. Chris would also go on to teach her how to defend herself with firearms and close quarters combat. He would then give her a custom made S.T.A.R.S knife and lighter.

Ada Wong

A mysterious woman with a mysterious past. In this take, she claims to be an FBI agent searching for Annette Birkin, one of the individuals responsible for unleashing the virus onto Raccoon City. However, there more to this story than she would like most to know of.

Sherry Birkin

A 12-year-old civilian found running around the police station. Even while taking refuge in the facility, it's believed that she is being pursued by a monster much worse than any zombie.

Annette Birkin

The mother of Sherry Birkin and wife of William Birkin. She is a scientist with Umbrella and is believed to be a key party for the outbreak in Raccoon City. It seems she is more absorded in her work than her daughter.

William Birkin

The father of Sherry Birkin and husband of Annette Birkin. He is a scientist with Umbrella and is believed to be a key party for the outbreak in Raccoon City. His wherabouts are currently unknown as he is believed to be deceased by most of the reminiaing survivors. However, he is neither here nor there.

Marvin Branagh

One of the few surviving officers in the Police Station. He assists the first run character in the beginning sections of the story.
