Characters

Jill Valentine

Former member of S.T.A.R.S Alpha Team. For the last two months, she worked closely with her fellow surviving colleagues to uncover Umbrella's secrets and reveal them to the public. All the while, she was dealing with the P.T.S.D brought about from the Mansion Incident. She intended to later join Chris and Barry in Europe for further investigation. But circumstances hastened her departure.

Brad Vickers

Former member of S.T.A.R.S Alpha Team. After the disbanding of his team, he continued investigating Umbrella with Jill after Chris, Barry, and Rebecca left the city. Being in the city like Jill, this paints a target on his back for the Nemesis to hunt him down too.

Carlos Olivera

A UBCS mercenary hired by Umbrella. Carlos faced a rough childhood as the fifth of seven brothers. Unfortunately, at least three were killed during his youth before he himself was captured by a paramilitary group. Only to be rescued by a communist group backed by Cuba or the Soviet Union. During this time, he was trained in guerrilla tactics before becoming a guerrilla leader himself. By the 1990s, Olivera's group was wiped out with him facing capture once again. This time, by Umbrella.

Nicholai Ginovaef

Leader of Carlos' UBCS unit. It is believed he served in the Soviet Union's army until its collapse. However, not much else is known about his retirement from the army.

Nemesis T-Type

The looming threat for both Jill and Brad. The Nemesis T-Type is a Bioweapon programmed to kill all remaining members of S.T.A.R.S and anyone who gets in his way of accomplishing that mission.
