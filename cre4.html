Characters

Leon Scott Kennedy

Protagonist of the game. After six years, Leon has become less naive and optimistic. However, he has grown to become a wise-cracking yet skilled agent in his field.

Ashley Graham

President Graham's twenty-year old daughter. On her way home from college, she was kidnapped by an unknown group and was last seen in a rural village in Europe. Why she was kidnapped remains to be said.

Ada Wong

The mysterious woman in red also makes her return in RE4. Only this time, her motives for taking part in the incident of rural Europe is a mystery to both Leon and the player.
