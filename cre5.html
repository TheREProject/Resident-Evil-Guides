Characters

Chris Redfield

Protagonist of the game. Much like anyone who has worked in his field for this lonig, he has become far more cynical than even Leon. The deaths of his S.T.A.R.S comrades and now Jill has taken a toll on him. However, he seems to be taking this mission in Africa for a certain reason...

Sheva Alomar

One of the newest members of the B.S.A.A's West African Division. She is Chris' partner through the game.

Josh Stone

Captain of the B.S.A.A's Delta Team of the West African Division.

Jill Valentine

Chris' former partner. Three years before the events of the game, she and Chris were following a lead on Ozwell E. Spencer's location. The duo instead found Spencer dead by the hands of Albert Wesker. In a losing battle, Jill sacrificed herself to save Chris. Something that continues to haunt him to this day.

Riccardo Irving

The man Chris and Sheva are searching for. It is believed he has intel on the current outbreak as well as its cause.

Albert Wesker

The source of many conflicts throughout the games. A power hungry madman who believes he is to be the saviour of this world. His reign came to an apparant end when Jill shoved him and herself down several stories. Although no bodies were found. You might wonder...
