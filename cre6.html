Characters

Leon Scott Kennedy

Over the years, Leon has become more stoic and a bit of a downer. After being forced to kill a zombiefied Adam to save Helena, both he and the latter are framed for murder and unleashing the C-Virus on Tall Oaks.

Chris Redfield

Captain of the B.S.A.A's Alpha Team. Six Months ago, he led a team through the Edonian Civil War up until a very tragic incident occourred. After that, Redfield went MIA for six months before he was found. Although reluctant to return to the field, he accepted that he cannot keep running away from what's happened, only to press on and accept responsibility.

Sherry Birkin

Due to the events of Raccooon City, Sherry' life became one of misery. While the G-Virus vaccine saved her from mutating like her father, it granted her an ability that the U.S Government wished to take advantag of. After over ten years of cativity, she was promised some sort of freedom if she decided to become an agent under their wing. Her current mission is to escort the man with the only known immunity to the C-Virus.

Ada Wong

Ada makes another return in this game once again playing a behind-the-scenes type of character. Although you may find it surprising that her existence influenced many of the games events including the C-Virus outbreak.

Helena Harper

A fellow United States agent. She was assigned to protect president Benford during his event at Tall Oaks University. She abandoned her post as the capmus was infected with the C-Virus. Her life was spared. However, she and Leon are now fugitives. The reason behind all this? All within the walls of the cathedral.

Piers Nivans

One of the few surviving members of Alpha Team from Edonia. He and the rest of the squad managed to trakc Chris down and bring him back to the field. Of course, this didn't come without its problems, and Piers needed to keep the Captain from getting to far over his head.

Jake Muller

A wise cracking mercenary who believes money is more important than anything. Upon learning of his immunity to the C-Virus, he took full advantage of this by charging 50 million USD for a pint of his blood. Despite his harsh and rough exterior, there is a reason for his immunity and his personality.
