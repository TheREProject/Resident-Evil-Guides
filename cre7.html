Characters

Ethan Winters

Protagonist of the game. A 31-year-old systems engineer from Los Angeles. By 2011, he married he soon to be wife Mia and moved to Texas. Unlike our previous protagonists, Ethan is fairly limited in combat.

Mia Winters

Ethan's wife of six years. Unbeknownst to her husband, Mia is a member of an organization who goes by the name of "The Connections." She disappeared on an assignment three years ago with no contact until July 2017. She was assumed deceased until she sent a message during the aformentioned month.

Jack Baker

An odd fellow with a tenacious will and prowess. He will hunt the ends of certain playing areas to ensure Ethan fails his quest.

Marguerite Baker

Jack Baker's equally kooky and animated wife. He love for insects suggest more than a natural interest...

Lucas Baker

The son of Jack and Marguerite. His intelligence with mechanics earned him several awards in his school days. However, he uses them for what he considers fun which unfortunately for Ethan and many others, is quite the contrary if not outright messed up.

Zoe Baker

The daughter of Jack and Marguerite and Lucas' sister. SHe appears to be the only normal member of the family. Although she's not exactly without her condition as well. That's why she seeks out Ethan's help.

Chris Redfield

Yes, this is Chris Redfield. I don't know what they were thinking during all this. But it was a rather odd design choice imo.
