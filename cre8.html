Characters

Ethan Winters

One of the few survivors of the Baker Incident, husband of Mia Winters, and father of Rose Winters. Ethan had hoped relocation would get him away from the craziness of Bioterrorism, but fate had another plan in mind. Just as things seemed somewhat normal, he loses his wife and has his daughter kidnapped. What for? He doesn't know.

Chris Redfield

Chris is now the leader of the Hound Wolf Squad and tasked to keep an eye on the Winters family. For reasons unknown to Ethan, Rose was kidnapped, and Mia was killed. Despite Ethan's questions, Chris refuses to answer. What could he be hiding?

Mia Winters

Ethan's wife and Rose's mother. Unlike Ethan, Mia would prefer to forget what happened at the Baker estate. However, something about her seems off. Much like last time, Ethan feels like he is being played of sorts.

Rosemary Winters

Daughter of Mia and Ethan Winters. As a baby, one would expect that there would be nothing of note about her. However, Chris Redfield and his squad believe otherwise...
