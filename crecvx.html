Characters

Claire Redfield

The younger sister of Chris Redfield. She shares her brother's bold bravery as shown in Resident Evil 2 and this game. After parting ways with Leon and Sherry. Her goal was nothing more than to find her brother. Lucky for her however, she is still good friends enough with Leon for her to have him do a favor for her.

Steve Burnside

A 17-year-old young man whom Claire meets on the island. It is unknown why he is there. But he seems rather detached from people as he considers them nothing but letdowns.

Alfred Ashford

Director of the Rockfort Island Prison and member of the Ashford Family. His grandfather was one of the three founding members of Umbrella. But unfortunately, Alfred while fairly smart did not inherit his ancestors' genius intellect unlike his twin sister Alexia.

Rodrigo Juan Raval

A member of the Umbrella Security Service (U.S.S) who captures Claire Redfield and is also her cell guard. Actions taken with him can greatly affect the rest of the game.

Chris Redfield

Former member of S.T.A.R.S Alpha Team and the older brother of Claire Redfield. After the events of the Mansion Incident, he began to investigate Umbrella with his fellow surviving S.T.A.R.S colleagues. Jill and Brad stayed in Raccoon City (you know how that went), Rebecca left the city, and he and Barry went to Europe. Thanks to Leon Kennedy, Chris was made known of Claire's capture and imprisonment on Rockfort Island.
