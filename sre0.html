Summary

July 23rd 1998, Special Tactics and Rescue Service (S.T.A.R.S) Bravo Team is investigating murder cases some occurring in the Arklay Mountains outside of Raccoon City. As they flew over the forest, their helicopter began to malfunction requiring a crash landing.

Upon exiting their crashed aircraft, the team discover an execution notice of Former Marine Second Lieutenant Billy Coen. Captain Enrico Marini (foolishy) ordered his subordinates to spit up and search the forest both for information on the murder cases and now Billy Coen.

While searching the forest, Rebecca discovers a train in the area and steps inside to investigate...

Join Rebecca Chambers in the mystery behind the events of the T-Virus Outbreak in the prequel to Resident Evil.
