Summary

On the night of July 24th 1998, Special Tactics and Rescue Service (S.T.A.R.S) Alpha Teams is investigating some bizzare murder cases occurring on the outskirts of Raccoon City as well as a search and rescue of their colleagues on S.T.A.R.S Bravo Team.

Upon landing and investigating, Alpha Team member Joseph Frost encouterned Bravo Team's helicopter along with the corpse of its pilot, Kevin Dooley. Not long after, several dogs pounce on the man mauling him to death. Once finishing Joseph off, the dogs began to set their sights on the remaining Alpha members Chris Redfield, Jill Valentine, Barry Burton, and Albert Wesker.

The four run through the forest as their helicopter pilot Brad Vickers flies away from the conflict. The four grounded Alpha members are forced to take refuge in a nearby manision. They begin to investigate the Residence and uncover the evils lurking within. The following events that occur would eventually be known in history as "The Mansion Incident."
