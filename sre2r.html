Summary

This reimagining of the hit 1998 title Resident Evil 2 takes us back to September 29th 1998. Rookie office Leon Scott Kennedy and Claire Redfield arrive in Raccoon City under different circumstances. The former received reports to stay away from the city due to the viral outbreak of the T-Virus. Due to his "save everyone" nature, Leon made his way to the city A.S.A.P. Meanwhile, Claire lost contact with her brother Chris causing her to arrive in the city. It is through this event that she and Leon would enter a world that they can never truly leave from again...
