Summary

Nearly four years after the death of Albert Wesker, yet another bioterrorist organization called Neo Umbrella has emerged unleashing the deadly C-Virus upon various populations around the world. And there is only one man with the anitbodies that can counteract it. Characters both new and old are involved in the new outbreak as they too uncover the evils and secrets hidden within their own country and beyond.

Resident Evil 6 features three (later four) stories involving Leon Kennedy, Chris Redfield, Sherry Birkin, and Ada Wong and their respectrive ordeals faced during he C-Virus Outbreak. Their stories intersect at certain points allowing for increaased immersion and co-op opportunities when allowed.
