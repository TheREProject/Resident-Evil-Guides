Summary

July 2017, Ethan Winters receives a message from his long missing wife, Mia Winters. Igniting the man's worry, he eventually gets a lead that on her last known location near Dulvey Louisiana. Wasting no time, Ethan makes the trip to the isolated land. Upon his arrival, he would find and lose far more than he bargained for. Nonetheless, Ethan would continue to press on.
