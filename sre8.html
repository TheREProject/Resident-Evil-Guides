Summary

Four years after the Baker Incident, Ethan and Mia Winters have been reloacated to a home in Europe. Since then, the couple had a daughter named Rose. One ordinary night, Mia was shot in the chest by an unidentified man before his team charged into the house. The head of this attack is none other than Chris Redfield. The man knocks Ethan to his knees and shoots Mia in the face. Rose and Ethan are then taken captive by Chris and his team. During escort, their vehicle was attacked and trashed. Rose now missing, Ethan set out into the nearby village to find his daughter and find the answers behind these bizarre events.
