Summary

Three months after escaping Raccoon City, Claire Redfield found a lead to her brother Chris Redfield's location. She broke into an Umbrella Facility in Paris but was captured by the company after a surprise attack by one of the few smart characters in the series. Now, Claire found herself on Rockfort Island, an island used by Umbrella. But not long after her sentencing, she awoke to the sound of explosions. Could it be rescue? The only way to know is to escape. A shadowy figure approaches her jail cell...
