Walkthrough

NOTE: Unlike the previous two titles, Resident Evil 3: Nemesis follows only one storyline. However, there are multiple endings. There are certain points in the game where the player must choose an option (dubbed "Live Selection.") Skipping these selections will not result in death. However, they can lead to taking a great amount of damage. At least two of these selections will determine which ending the player receives.
