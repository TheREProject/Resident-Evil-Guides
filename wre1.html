Weapons

Combat Knife

Your go-to melee weapon. Unless you're a very skilled player, this weapon is practically useless and barring one situation, it's meant to be used as a last resort.

Beretta

A 9mm Handgun used by both Chris and Jill. Best used on weaker enemies.

Shotgun

A shotgun used by both Chris and Jill. Best used on stronger enemies or headshotting zombies. For best use, aim up at a zombie's head and fire for guaranteed headshots.

Magnum

A magnum used by both Chris and Jill. Best used on stronger enemies and bosses. Unfortunately, ammo is very limited compared to shotgun and handgun ammo. So use sparingly.

Bazooka (Grenade Launcher)

A weapon used by Jill only. Ammo types can vary by explosive, flame, and acid shells. Ammo is not as limited as the magnum. However, it is still rather rare. It is great to use as an alternate weapon when shotgun ammo is low.

Flamethrower

A weapon used by Chris only. As it can only be used in specific locations, it is best to use this weapon before switching back to your permanent weapons.
