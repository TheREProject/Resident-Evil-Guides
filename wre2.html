Weapons

Combat Knife

Your go-to melee weapon. Like the previous entry, unless you're a very skilled player, this weapon is practically useless.

Claire's Handgun

A 9mm Handgun used by Claire. It has a thirteen clip capacity Best used on weaker enemies.

Leon's Handgun

A handgun used by Leon. While it boasts an eighteen bullet capacity, it is still best used for weaker enemies.

Bowgun

A crossbow used by Claire. It is not a very good weapon to use except against zombies. You can use it to save handgun bullets if needed.

Shotgun

A weapon used by Leon. It will be your go-to power weapon for enemies of all sorts. Unlike its predecessor, it can later be upgraded to demolish crowds and bosses.

Grenade Launcher

A weapon used by Claire. Like the previous entry, ammo comes from grenade, acid, or flame rounds. Some rounds are stronger against certain enemies than other. So choose wisely.

Colt Python

A special handgun used only by Claire if certain conditions are met. It uses handgun ammo.

Magnum

A high grade handgun used by Leon. Like the previous entry, ammo is more scarce than shotgun ammo. However, it can be upgraded to pack an even greater punch.

Flamethrower

A weapon used by Leon. Unlike the previous entry, the flamthrower can be kept from the moment its's obtained. However, by the time you obtain it, you've likely got the upgraded shotgun and magnum. Still, it has a use against foes who are weak to fire.

Sparkshot

A special weapon used by Claire. Electrocutes enemies or destroys them in one hit depending on luck!

Sub-machine gun

An automatic weapon that can be obtained partway through the campaign or as a special weapon upon certain completion. If obtaining using the former method as the A character, it WON'T be present for the B character.
