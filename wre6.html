Weapons

Note: Due to the nature of the game, most weapoons types virtually function the same not counting the semi automatic rifles and bolt action rifles. I will only cover the unique charcater exclusive weapons and/or abilities.

  • For each campaign, a character can have a handgun, shotgun, sniper rifle, grenades, and assault rifle. The campaign you play determines which type you will have or obtain through the game.
  • The Grenade Launcher and its variants are available to everyone except Leon and Helena.
  • Magnums are reserved for Leon, Helena, Jake, and Sherry.
  • Knives are reserved for Leon and Chris only.





Character Exclusive Weapons

Hydra

Helena's exclusive weapon. It's not as good as the previous hydra. However, it's a good shotgun to use until you get the real one or are running low on regular shotgun shells.

Anti-Material Rifle

A Piers Nivans Exclusive weapon. It functions as a bolt action sniper rifle but is slower although packs a bigger punch. Don't use it up close!

MP-AF

Piers' exclusive machine pistol. It's a great substitute for the assault rifle when fighting regular enemies.

Stun Rod

Sherry's exclusive melee weapon. It can be charged to unleash stronger attacks as well as alter Sherry's maneuvering. When charging, Sherry's movement will be slower. So, take care when using this.

Hand-To-Hand

Jake's exclusive melee "weapon." All RE characters are proficient in unarmed combat. Jake takes this to a different level where his move tend to be very familiar. It's best learned by experimenting on unarmed enemies.

Crossbow

Ada's exclusive weapon. Use the regular bolts to stealth kill enemies or the explosive rounds to charge in loud. Each use requires a reload.
